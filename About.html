My name is Sebastian, and I am currently studying Mechanical Engineering at the University of Toronto. My main areas of focus are Mechatronics and Energy, two topics that have interested me all throughout my life. When I was 15, I built my first desktop PC, and while is was just a symptom of my affinity for technology rather than the cause, it really opened my eyes to the vast potentials of modern technology.
